a little about me

Sorry another admin person joining the tech bandwagon with aspirations to become a UX designer. For a little under a year now, I’ve been studying as much as I can about UX/UI, and completed a many different courses online, as well as attending many workshops. From this, I have a good knowledge of UX processes as well as skills. I pride myself on being adaptable, and having the desire to constantly grow, learn and evolve my skills.


principles and aims:
    What is your working style?  I’m task-orientated and get a sense of achievement from completing tasks, it's nice to see the entire project through. also better when you're learning as you go.
    What’s your design philosophy?  I follow the hicks law/K.I.S.S principle, it's best not to overcomplicate things.
    Who do you aspire to be in 10yrs time?  I would like to working for a creative environment with people who are passionate about their work.
