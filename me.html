I am aide ohirein

I'm a London based UX Designer & illustrator, my design style follows hicks law/K.I.S.S principle, it's best not to overcomplicate things. I am proficient in using most prototyping systems including Adobe XD, Figma and Sketch, and as you can see in my site, HTML and CSS as well ^_^. I have a good knowledge of UX processes and pride myself on the desire to constantly grow, learn and evolve my skills.
