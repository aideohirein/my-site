    • Persona:

    TARGET AUDIENCE: Healthy, Socially conscious 20 to 40 years old UK based Mixed ethnicity, focusing on people of ethnic backgrounds as there is a greater need.

    DESCRIPTION: Athletic women, most likely high Gym users, tend to be highly active on social media, with a high followings, not necessarily influencers.

    INTERVIEW: 5 interviews with a mix of people aged between 20 and 40, asking them what they thought of the userbility of the site.

    CATEGORIES: From the interviews I’ve discovered that the main issues were the as follows:

  • The site was cold and too medical
  • It didn’t invoke enough emotion from it’s user
  • That the general site was far too wordy
  • Site is awkward to use
  • The appointments were too far, (if you booked in Oct you may not get an appointment until Jan/Feb the next year)
