give blood

Client: NHS - “ Give Blood” website, for people to gain information on how to give blood. Primary objectives: Simplify the the current site of NHS’s “ Give Blood” website Target Audience: See Persona for site Feature List (Product requirements): Logo Nav Why give blood Demand for different blood types Black, Asian and minority ethnic communities Why we need more black donors Rare blood types Who can give blood Can I give blood? Getting an appointment to give blood Health and Eligibility Travel considerations Occupation considerations Men who have sex with men Donors and disability The donation process Registering online Preparing to give blood What happens on the day After your donation Where to give blood? (Does to search page) News and Campaigns Body Resgistations News, features and updates Other forms of donations Product Inspiration: https://www.redcrossblood.org/ https://www.donateblood.com.au/ Deliverables: • Wireframes for client approval. • High fidelity prototype. • User Testing - We’ll be using Hallway testing & unmoderated remote testing. • UI Assets for developers. Persona TARGET AUDIENCE Healthy, Socially concious 20 to 40 years old UK based Mixed ethnicity, focusing on people of ethnic backgrounds as there is a greater need. DESCRIPTION Athletic women, most likely high Gym users, tend to be highly active on social media, with a high followings, not neccessarly infulencers. Research and interviews INTERVIEW 5 interviews with a mix of people aged between 20 and 40, asking them what they thought of the userbilty of the site. CATEGORIES From the interviews I’ve discovered that the main issues were the as follows: - The site was cold and too medical - It didn’t invoke enough emotion from it’s user - That the general site was far too wordy - Site is awkward to use - The appointments were too far, (if you booked in Oct you may not get an appointment until Jan/Feb the next year)
